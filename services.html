S.01

QRA & Inspection Equipment

We supply, install, and support the instruments that semiconductor and electronics reliability labs depend on — from sample preparation through failure analysis documentation. Our principals cover the full analytical chain.

Whether you're running incoming QRA on a new package, performing failure analysis on a line excursion, or setting up a new metallography lab, we can specify the right equipment, source it, and get your engineers trained on it.

  • Optical metallography systems — mounting, grinding, polishing, imaging
  • Bond pull and die shear testers — JEDEC and AEC-Q compliant
  • Scanning acoustic microscopy (SAM) for delamination and void detection
  • Cross-sectioning and precision micro-milling systems
  • Calibration, installation qualification, and PM contracts included

S.02

PCB Design, Assembly & Repair

Our Dasmariñas, Cavite facility handles the full PCB lifecycle — from schematic capture to functional prototype delivery, and from SMT assembly through board-level field repair. We work directly with EMS companies, OEM engineering teams, and university research labs.

We can accept customer-supplied Gerbers or take a board from concept to finished assembly. Our rework cell handles BGA reballing, QFP replacement, and conformal coating repair on production boards.

  • Schematic capture, multilayer layout, impedance-controlled routing
  • 4-layer and higher board capability with DFM review
  • SMT stencil print, pick-and-place, reflow, and AOI inspection
  • BGA reballing and component-level rework on production boards
  • Full QA traveler with every prototype or repair delivery

Probe Card Solutions

As the authorized Philippine distributor for Nidec SV Probe, we supply probe cards for wafer test and characterization — and provide the cleaning materials and field engineering support to keep them performing to spec.

We support cantilever, vertical, and MEMS probe heads across a range of wafer sizes and pitch geometries. Our engineering team can assist with initial setup, needle alignment verification, and contact resistance qualification.

  • Cantilever, vertical, and MEMS probe card types
  • Probe cleaning media — tungsten and palladium compatible
  • Field engineering for card setup, needle alignment, and verification
  • Contact resistance and performance qualification support
  • Replacement tips, repair, and refurbishment coordination

S.05

Reliability & Environmental Test

The chambers, ovens, and systems used to stress-screen, qualify, and characterize semiconductor and electronics products — supplied, installed, calibrated, and supported locally. We cover the major reliability test modalities required by JEDEC, IPC, AEC-Q, and automotive customers.

We work with labs that are setting up new qualification capabilities and with production floors that need burn-in and screening capacity. All equipment comes with installation support and PM contracts.

  • Air-to-air thermal shock chambers — per JEDEC JESD22-A104
  • HAST (Highly Accelerated Stress Test) and autoclave / PCT
  • Temperature-humidity-bias (THB) combined environment chambers
  • Burn-in ovens and boards for HTOL and HTSL screening
  • Installed, calibrated, PM-contracted — all in-country
