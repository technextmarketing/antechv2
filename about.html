03 / IPC Class 3 Capability

Highest-reliability assembly standard

IPC Class 3 is the most demanding category in electronic assembly — required for aerospace, defense, medical, and life-critical applications.

Class 3
IPC-A-610 · Highest Reliability

While Class 1 covers general electronics and Class 2 covers dedicated service, IPC Class 3 demands zero-tolerance workmanship for high-reliability products where failure is not an option. Antech-Enviro's assembly capability fully meets Class 3 criteria — making us the right partner for medical devices, automotive control systems, and industrial instrumentation.

Zero solder voids tolerance — X-ray verified solder joint integrity on every BGA and bottom-termination component.
100% AOI coverage — Automated optical inspection after paste, placement, and reflow stages.
Certified IPC operators — All assembly technicians trained and certified to IPC-A-610 Class 3 criteria.
Full traceability — Component lot tracking, process parameter logging, and serialized test records retained per IPC-1782.
Conformal coating & potting — Available for high-humidity, vibration, or chemical-exposure environments.
04 / Testing & Inspection

Equipment highlights

Multi-stage inspection and testing ensures zero-defect delivery — every board is checked at paste, placement, reflow, and final stages.

Solder Paste Inspection (SPI)
3D paste volume and height measurement after stencil printing. Catches volume, alignment, and bridging defects before component placement.
Automated Optical Inspection (AOI)
Post-placement and post-reflow AOI using multi-angle imaging to detect missing, misaligned, tombstoned, and bridged components.
X-Ray Inspection (AXI)
2D and 3D X-ray for hidden solder joints under BGAs, QFNs, and LGAs. Checks void percentage, fill level, and head-in-pillow defects.
In-Circuit Testing (ICT)
Bed-of-nails or flying-probe ICT verifies component values, placement polarity, shorts, and opens against the board netlist.
Functional Testing (FCT)
Power-on functional test using customer-supplied fixtures or our in-house test development service to verify end-product behaviour.
Reliability Stress Testing
HALT, burn-in, thermal cycling, and vibration testing available through our QRA equipment partner network for validation builds.

07 / FAQ

Answers made clear

Common questions about working with Antech-Enviro.

Where is Antech-Enviro located and do you ship nationwide?
We are headquartered in Parañaque, Philippines. We deliver across all major industrial zones and offer international shipments via trusted freight partners. Contact us for regional and export availability.
What is the minimum order quantity for PCB assembly?
We have no minimum order quantity. We support prototype builds of a single unit through to volume production runs. Pricing scales with volume, but we never turn away low-quantity jobs that matter to your development cycle.
Do you offer turnkey or consignment assembly?
We offer both. Turnkey means we procure all components on your behalf from qualified distributors. Consignment (kitted) means you supply the parts and we provide assembly, inspection, and testing only. We recommend turnkey for faster lead times and single-source accountability.
Can you work with our existing PCB design files?
Yes. We accept Gerber (RS-274X), ODB++, IPC-2581, and most EDA tool exports. Our engineers review your files for DFM issues before committing to production. If we spot a problem, we'll flag it before it becomes a costly defect.
What industries do you primarily serve?
Our primary customers are in semiconductor manufacturing (fabs and OSAT), EMS companies, automotive electronics suppliers, and medical device manufacturers operating in the Philippines. We also serve industrial automation, power electronics, and telecommunications equipment makers.
How do I get a quote?
Use our contact form or email us directly. For PCB assembly, send your Gerber files, BOM, and quantity. For equipment or consumables, describe the product or application. We aim to respond within one business day with a preliminary quote.

Ready to work with a trusted partner?

Talk to our team about your PCB assembly, inspection equipment, or technical service requirements. Response within one business day.
